February 2025 Continuous Mix

Published by ambalek on 3rd March 2025

February studio experiments, continuous mix.

This mix is a set of tracks I worked on in February, several involving experiments with MIDI control over guitar pedals.

The first track in the mix was made by putting several eurorack sounds through Silhouette. Silhouette accepts multiple inputs, so you can move between them and add tails by blurring the signal. The source sounds were from the OP-1 Field's string instrument, looped and mangled by Morphagene. I did some manual control over Morphagene so I could make it feel like the strings were being plucked and mangled. I used Silhouette to blend the string sounds with a Just Friends voice, and another drone made by Mangrove and Three Sisters.
