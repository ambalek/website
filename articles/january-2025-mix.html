January 2025 Continuous Mix

Published by ambalek on 7th February 2025

January studio experiments, continuous mix.

I collected together the various studio sessions I've had in January and created a continuous mix that you can download.

This month involved learning more about Mannequins modules, mixing OP-1 sampling with eurorack, and relying more on looping than sequencing.

That's not to say looping is better than sequencing, it's just I want to dedicate some time to creating purely sample-based work for the next album I work on.

Over the last few days I've been attempting to make more complex networks of guitar pedals controlled by MIDI CC values, so I was hoping that might lead somewhere quite different to this month's music making.

Next month: Potentially a longer mix.
